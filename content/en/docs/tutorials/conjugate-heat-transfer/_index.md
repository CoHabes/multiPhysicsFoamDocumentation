Conjugate heat transfer (CHT) couples fluid and solid thermal domains through a
shared interface, allowing heat conduction across region boundaries.

## Cases

- [Flow Over Heated Plate](flow-over-heated-plate/) — classical CHT validation case with a heated flat plate
- [Shell and Tube Heat Exchanger](shell-and-tube-heat-exchanger/) — multi-region CHT in a shell-and-tube geometry
