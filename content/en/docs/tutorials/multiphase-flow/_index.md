Multiphase flow tutorials demonstrate interface-coupled simulations of two
immiscible fluids with a sharp capillary interface.

## Cases

- [2D Rising Bubble](2d-rising-bubble/) — 2D axisymmetric rising bubble benchmark
- [3D Duineveld Rising Bubble](3d-duineveld-rising-bubble/) — 3D rising bubble comparison with Duineveld experiments
- [3D Oscillating Bubble](3d-oscillating-bubble/) — 3D freely oscillating bubble
