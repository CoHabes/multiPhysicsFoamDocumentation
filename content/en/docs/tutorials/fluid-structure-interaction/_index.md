Fluid-structure interaction (FSI) tutorials couple a fluid solver with a
structural solid solver across a moving interface using the ALE framework.

## Cases

- [Beam in Cross Flow](beam-in-cross-flow/) — flexible beam deflected by a laminar cross-flow
- [Hron-Turek FSI3](hron-turek-fsi3/) — benchmark FSI3 case from Hron & Turek (2006)
